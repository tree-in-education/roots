Part 3: Real Life

Now let’s talk about the Chicago Gang Database that is being used by the Chicago Police Department; another agent that doesn’t really follow those guidelines we laid out, which also corrupts the power of databases.

We aren't able to know too much about it, but here are some things that we do know:

  • - It includes information about 128,000 people and counting
  • - It is inaccurate in that it includes 13 people who are supposedly 118 years old — and two others listed as 132. For the record, the oldest person to ever live lived to be 122 years old.

It is also inaccurate in that the CPD doesn’t even know exactly where their data is really even coming from; Police add people to the database for reasons like their race and the neighborhoods where they lived, or if they suspect that they are in a gang or have gang tattoos
