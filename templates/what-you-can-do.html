Even though it’s hard to know if you’re in the Chicago Gang Database, it’s still possible by leveraging the Freedom of Information Act! This link takes you through the steps: http://erasethedatabase.com/foia/

Summary of what you have to do:

Step 1: Send Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) request using email template from the above website to the Chicago Police Department (CPD):

Step 2: Wait for a response

Step 3: Fight the denial, or review your documents. To fight the denial, take the steps mentioned in the website above.
