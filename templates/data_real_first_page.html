Part 1: What is Data?

We all know about Social Media websites/platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter; E-commerce websites like Amazon and Ebay, and even music streaming services like Spotify and Pandora. All these services collect huge amounts of data, you’re data, and do something with it.

In this learning module, you will learn more about how that data is collected, brought together, and analyzed in the form of databases, as well as what the ethical implications behind that may be, especially as it relates to something that might hit home a little closer than you might think: the Chicago Gang Database.

As we discuss databases, we’ll also talk about the different types of databases that exist, and how those might inform the conclusions that may be made from analyzing those databases.
