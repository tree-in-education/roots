Part 2: Intro to Databases

Again returning back to the information ladder, the goal of databases is to transform data into information in order to generate knowledge that can be used for decision making.

We're working with the part of the information ladder that goes from data to information:

They do this by taking data, putting the data into context, and providing tools for aggregation and analysis so we can produce knowledge.

In simple terms, Databases are collections of tables, organized in columns and rows.
