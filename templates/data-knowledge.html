Part 1: What is data?

Once we have put our data into context, and we aggregated and analyzed it, we can eventually use it to make decisions about something.

We can say that this consumption of information produces knowledge.

What can knowledge then be used for?

When people combine their knowledge and experience to produce a deeper understanding of a topic, this is called creating wisdom. This wisdom can be used to make decisions, set policies, and even spark innovation.
